  • Monitoring Construction Schools

    we work during the gap periodwhen children are forgotten after emergency response is complete, bringing hope and empowerment to generations of children and entire communities

    Know More

  • Context

    After schools are selected, HHF and its partners will contract with construction companies to begin the build process. Rebuilding a school can take anywhere from 2 to 8 months, depending on the size and other factors. Many schools in different communities & countries will be under construction at the same time. HHF is currently tracking the progress of 23 schools, each at different phases of the construction process. The construction progress is monitored through weekly calls that are logged in a spreadsheet. Aside from these weekly touch points, some partners provide a midterm report that details the school’s progress at the halfway point and most provide a final report. Tracking progress of each school is very important for HHF, given the scale of their operations and the need to identify escalation issues, such as delays that impact timeline and payment schedules. HHF also provides reporting updates to donors, who are very interested in understanding the progress of each school.

    Know More

  • Challenge

    Monitoring the construction of each school is a very manual process, involving phone calls to partners who connect with workers on the ground and manually uploading information into spreadsheets. Partners have internet access in their offices, but labor workers do not or have very limited internet access at the school’s location. Additionally, not all of the workers on the ground speak English (HHF has to consider language barriers in different regions when requesting reports and updates). HHF needs a way to collect information from both labor workers and partners who have smartphones at each school’s location. This information will provide accurate and frequent updates that will greatly benefit HHF and their ability to manage the end-to-end build process. HHF would like an app that allows for streamlined reporting and easy tracking, while also incorporating a simple way to share these updates and reports with its donors.

About Us

We are dedicated to rebuilding safe, resilient schools and to restoring hope and opportunity in the lives of children after natural disasters. I’ve found that if instead of only supporting communities during first response we create a sustained response throughout their recovery by rebuilding schools the ripple effects on children’s emotional healing, their potential job opportunities, and the overall hopefulness of the community is incredible.
